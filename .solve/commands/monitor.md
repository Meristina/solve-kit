the loop). `$MISSION` = `$ARGUMENTS`.

2. **Invoke Officer 5** (`agents/officer-5-monitor.md`, or adopt its role): MEASURE

steer decision (with loop-back to the right phase if it misses). Mirror the user's
language. The full deliverable then goes to `/solve.inspect` (FINAL).
